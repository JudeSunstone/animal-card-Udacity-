Cat

When a family cat died in ancient Egypt, family members would mourn by shaving off their eyebrows.

  • Scientific Name: Felis silvestris catus or Felis catus
  • Average lenght: 46 cm without tail
  • Average lifespan: 4-17 years
  • Habitat: across the world

The domestic cat is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal. Cats are often valued by humans for companionship and for their ability to hunt vermin. There are more than 70 cat breeds, though different associations proclaim different numbers according to their standards.
